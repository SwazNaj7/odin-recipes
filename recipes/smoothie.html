Healthy Trushake Smoothie

Steps:

  1. Get Container
  2. Add 1 Trushake
  3. Add 1 banana
  4. Add Lasco of any flavour
  5. Add sunflower seeds
  6. Add chia seeds
  7. Add pumpkin seeds
  8. Add oats/li>
  9. Add cinammon powder
  10. A little nutmeg/vanilla
  11. Add ice
  12. Blend till smooth
