Delicious Seasoned Omlette - Jamaican Style

Steps:

  1. Prepare Bowl and grab a fork
  2. Crack 2 Eggs in the bowl
  3. Beat the eggs until it become foamy
  4. Add All Purpose Seasoning
  5. Add Black Pepper
  6. Add Garlic Powder
  7. Add a little vinegar
  8. Cut up tomatoes and add it to bowl
  9. Add onions
  10. Add Pepper Sauce (optional)
  11. Cut up green peppers and add a little cayenne pepper
  12. Mix Eggs
